How to cook Pasta Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 8-10 slices bacon, cut into 1/2 inche pieces
  • 8 1/2 cups water
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 lb spaghetti or linguine
  • 1 1/4 cups finely greated parmesan cheese
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 1 tsp each salt and pepper
  • Chopped fresh parsley

Instructions:

  1. Add bacon and 1/2 cup of the water to a large non-stick skillet and bring to a simmer over medium-high heat.
  2. Allow to simmer until water evaporates about 6-7 minutes, then reduce heat to medium-low and continue to cook until bacon is brown and crisp, about 6-8 minutes longer
  3. Place a fine mesh strainer over a bowl then pour bacon into stariner while reserving about 1 tbsp of the rendered fat in pan.
  4. Pour into a medium mixing bowl then add 1 Tsp rendered bacon fat(Drippings in bowl)
  5. Meanwhile, bring 8 cups of water to a boil in a large dutch oven. While pasta is boiling, set a colander in a large bowl
  6. Carefully, drain al dente pasta into colander bowl, while reserving pasta water in bowl.Measure out 1 cup hot pasta water and discard remaining water.
  7. Slowly pour and whisk 1/2 cup pasta water into egg mixture, then slowly poyr mixture over pasta while tossing to coat.
  8. Let pasta rest, tossing frequently,2-4 minutes until sauce has thickened slighlty and coats pasta.
